Hey you! My name is Frankie,
I’m a space shooting producer.

I bring art to space through spacial photoshoots. Our galaxy opens up a whole new world of potential for the expansion and expression of art in many forms. Making that potential a reality keeps me energized and feeds my imagination. It’s an entirely different perspective for an artist - for a human being! - to have the whole galaxy as your canvas. It makes you realize how special and unique our planet is, and how tiny and insignificant we are in the expanse of the cosmos. It brings completely new elements to my work, and I’m immensely grateful for the opportunities I have. If my work creates a shift in perspective for just one person, then I’ve created beauty that moves the world.
